How It Works

Five clear steps to a flawless finish

01

Free Site Inspection

We visit your property (no cost, no obligation) to assess surface condition, measure square meterage, and recommend the ideal coating system for your specific walls.

02

Fixed Quote — Same Day

03

Material Delivery

Once you accept, we deliver the specified Gamazine / Glamour Coat materials directly to your site. Only when they arrive do you pay — never before.

Professional Application

Our certified applicators arrive with the right tools and follow a strict multi‑layer process: primer, base coat, texture application, and protective top sealing.
