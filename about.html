Who We Are

Over a decade of protecting
South African homes

Every wall we coat is a structure someone calls home, a business someone built, a dream someone saved for. That responsibility shapes every decision we make.

Global Gamazine Suppliers was born in 2012 from a simple, unshakeable conviction: premium wall protection should never come with financial fear. Our zero‑deposit policy — you pay only when the materials arrive on your site — has earned the trust of over 1,200 clients across every province in South Africa.

We are not a general contracting firm that occasionally applies Gamazine. Our applicators are career specialists trained exclusively in Gamazine chemistry, weather‑responsive application techniques, and texture calibration. When they arrive at your property, they bring a decade of focused experience — not a toolbox of guesswork.

From a single crew in Kya Sands, we now coordinate teams from Limpopo to the Cape, maintaining the same exacting standards whether we're coating a two‑bedroom cottage or a retail complex. Every project carries a signed 15‑year guarantee because we believe craftsmanship should be underwritten, not just promised.
