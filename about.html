Who We Are

Over a decade of protecting South African homes — one wall at a time.

Global Gamazine Suppliers is South Africa's premier wall coating specialist, founded on the belief that every homeowner and business deserves a premium finish without financial risk. Our zero‑deposit policy — you pay only when materials arrive on site — has built a reputation that no marketing campaign ever could.

From a single crew in Randburg in 2012, we have grown into a nationwide operation completing projects from Cape Town to Limpopo. Our applicators are not contractors — they are craftspeople trained specifically in Gamazine's unique chemistry and application demands, ensuring every finish achieves maximum durability and aesthetic precision.

Every project we accept comes with a signed 15‑year guarantee. That is not a marketing figure — it reflects the quality of materials we source and the standards our team refuses to compromise on.
