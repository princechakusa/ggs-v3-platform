Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is Gamazine?

Gamazine is a premium wall coating made from quartz stone, acrylic resin, and UV‑stable pigments. It creates a durable, waterproof, crack‑resistant finish that lasts up to 15 years. It can be applied to both exterior and interior walls.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

No. We never take deposits. You only pay once the materials are delivered to your site — not before.

How long does the application take?

Most residential projects are completed within 2–4 days. Large commercial jobs may take longer, but we always provide a clear timeline during your quote.

Is the coating really waterproof?

Yes. Gamazine forms a seamless, waterproof barrier that prevents moisture penetration, damp, and mould growth. It is tested in SA's harshest weather conditions.

What colours are available?

We offer over 20 standard colours and can produce custom tints to match any architectural palette. All pigments are UV‑stable to prevent fading.

Does the 15‑year guarantee really hold?

Absolutely. Every project comes with a signed 15‑year written guarantee covering material defects and application quality. We stand behind every wall we coat.
